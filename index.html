Introduction

The Docbkx Wrapper Plugin wraps the docbkx-maven-plugin and the htmlfilter-site-maven-plugin such that they easily produce Maven site documentation from DocBook source that will fit in with the Doxia-generated site reports produced by the site plugin.

Goals Overview

The Docbkx Wrapper Plugin has one goal:

  • docbkx-wrapper:generate runs docbkx:generate-xhtml and then runs htmlfilter-site:merge on the resulting files.
